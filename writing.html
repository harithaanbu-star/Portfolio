I Built an AI That Could Save Your Life in 60 Seconds After a Snake Bite

The origin story behind VenomGuard AI: a late-night emergency call about a snakebite in a rural Tamil Nadu village with no clear answers — no known species, no certainty on antivenom availability, no clear next step. The piece walks through why snakebite deaths are so often preventable in theory but not in practice, how the tool turns a photo and a few patient details into a personalised treatment-window estimate, and why the system is built to escalate straight to an alarm for high-risk species rather than hedge with generic advice.
